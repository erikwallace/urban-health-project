Project Connect is a program under the umbrella of Cincinnati Public Schools. It serves the homeless child population in the Greater Cincinnati area. [Project Connect is] an organization that focuses on the educational needs of the homeless children, an area that is often overlooked. The goal of Project Connect is to advocate and provide services for children who have neither a voice nor a choice in being homeless. They accomplish this by: clearing obstacles for attendance, arranging transportation, distributing school supplies and backpacks, arranging access to lunch, providing tutoring, encouraging parents to stay involved in their child's life, referring parents to other educational resources, and hosting a 6-week summer camp for the kids. Beyond the services they provide, they let the children know that there are people that care about them. They all had their different personalities and different styles, and it was incredibly enjoyable to get to know them. I hope I managed to make some impact on them, and I hope that impact helps them in their future lives.

Working with homeless children was one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. At times, it was also challenging but, overall, it was a great experience to work with these kids and get to know them and their stories.
